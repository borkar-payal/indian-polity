### Why the term *Union* and not *Federation*?

Dr. B.R. Ambedkar clarified that:
- Indian federation is **not the result of an agreement** among states  
- States have **no right to secede**  
- The **Union is indestructible**, while **states are destructible**

| Aspect | India |
|-----|------|
| Nature | Federal with unitary bias |
| Secession | Not permitted |

## 6. Administration of Union Territories

### General Rule (Article 239)

## 9A. State Reorganisation Committees / Commissions

### 1. Dhar Commission (1948)
- Chairman: **Justice S.K. Dhar**
- Recommended administrative convenience over language
- Rejected immediate linguistic reorganisation

### 2. JVP Committee (1949)
**Members**
- Jawaharlal Nehru  
- Vallabhbhai Patel  
- Pattabhi Sitaramayya  

**Conclusion**
- Linguistic states only when national unity permits  
- Rejected language as sole basis  
- Resulted in creation of **Andhra State (1953)**

- H.N. Kunzru
